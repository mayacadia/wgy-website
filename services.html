01

Premium Airfare Rates

WGY Travel secures the best rates in business and first-class air tickets, leveraging agreements with major airlines worldwide. This access to private and bulk fares can result in savings of up to 40% on published fares.

02

Personalized Travel Planning

The agency offers both personal and corporate travel planning, ensuring each itinerary is customized to the client's preferences and requirements. 03

Complimentary Upgrades

When available, WGY Travel provides complimentary upgrades to enhance the travel experience, adding value to the client's journey.

VIP Travel Access
